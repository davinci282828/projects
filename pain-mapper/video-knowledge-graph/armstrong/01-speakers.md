# Speaker Context

## David Senra
Host of the Founders Podcast — obsessively reads 400+ biographies of history's greatest entrepreneurs and distills their lessons for builders. Known for pattern spotting across centuries of business history and connecting founder psychology across eras.

## Brian Armstrong
Co-founder & CEO of Coinbase. Built the first regulated on-ramp for Bitcoin in the US, turned a nights-and-weekends Y Combinator prototype into a public company worth tens of billions. Thesis: crypto = global financial infrastructure that increases economic freedom. Also founded New Limit (longevity biotech) exploring epigenetic reprogramming.
