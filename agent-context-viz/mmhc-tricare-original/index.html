Understanding Your Benefits

Does TRICARE Cover Mental Health?

Yes. TRICARE covers outpatient mental health services including unlimited therapy sessions when medically necessary, with no annual session caps for active-duty families. Coverage applies to all TRICARE-eligible beneficiaries: active-duty service members, retirees, National Guard/Reserve members, and their dependents enrolled in TRICARE Select or TRICARE Reserve Select.

TRICARE mental health benefits are among the most comprehensive in the insurance industry. Telehealth therapy is covered at the same rate as in-person visits, which means military families can access specialized care regardless of duty station location. No referral is required for outpatient mental health under most TRICARE Select plans — you can self-refer directly to in-network providers like MMHC.

MMHC's military-experienced therapists provide evidence-based treatment through secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth sessions. Unlike traditional practices with 2–4 week wait times, MMHC prioritizes rapid scheduling for new TRICARE patients following coverage verification.
