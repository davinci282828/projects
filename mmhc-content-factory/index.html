📱 How To Post These

Instagram (Organic Posts)

  1. Tap ⬇ on the image you want (or "Download All Approved")
  2. Images save to your phone/computer
  3. Open Instagram → tap + → select the image
  4. Tap the caption preview on any card → it copies to clipboard
  5. Paste as caption. Done.

Meta Ads Manager (Paid Ads)

  1. Go to adsmanager.facebook.com
  2. Create campaign → choose objective (Traffic or Conversions)
  3. At ad level: upload the downloaded image
  4. Paste the caption as Primary Text
  5. Set CTA button to "Book Now" or "Learn More"
  6. Target: New York State, ages 25-55, interests: mental health, therapy, wellness

LinkedIn

  1. Text-only posts perform best for thought leadership
  2. Copy text from the LinkedIn Strategy Doc
  3. Paste into LinkedIn → post as text. Images optional.
