TRICARE therapy coverage

Does TRICARE cover therapy?

Yes. TRICARE covers outpatient therapy — and TRICARE Select often makes it very affordable for military families. Active-duty family members pay $0 copay for in-network behavioral health visits. Retirees typically pay $15–$35 per session with no 20% coinsurance surprises when in-network.

Military families come to Manhattan Mental Health Counseling for therapy for:

  • Combat-related PTSD and complex trauma
  • Military Sexual Trauma (MST)
  • Deployment stress including hypervigilance and emotional numbing
  • Anxiety about transitions, PCS moves, or separations
  • Depression during or after service
  • Relationship challenges unique to military families
  • Career transition from military to civilian life
  • Caregiver stress for wounded warrior families

TRICARE Mental Health Coverage Details

TRICARE plans serve over 9.6 million beneficiaries and provide comprehensive mental health coverage that often exceeds civilian insurance.

  • $0 copay for active-duty family members (in-network)
  • No referral required for outpatient behavioral health
  • Unlimited sessions when medically necessary
  • Telehealth covered at same rate as in-person
  • No annual session caps
  • Catastrophic cap limits out-of-pocket costs
🧠

How many sessions does TRICARE cover?

TRICARE has no preset annual limits on therapy sessions. Coverage continues as long as it remains medically necessary. Think of therapy like physical training — you don't stop because you had one good workout. Many of our military patients start with a specific challenge and stay because consistent support keeps them more resilient. There is no preset limit.

Evidence-based approaches for military populations:

  • Cognitive Processing Therapy (CPT) — gold standard for PTSD
  • EMDR — Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing
  • Prolonged Exposure (PE) — for combat-related trauma
  • CBT adapted for military culture
  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) for values-based living
  • Military family systems therapy
