What teletherapy is (and what it isn’t)

When life is loud, you need something steady—not another complicated process.

  • Teletherapy is therapy delivered via secure video sessions.
  • It’s a real appointment with a licensed therapist, just online.
  • It’s not a “quick fix” or a script—it’s a consistent space to work through what’s going on.

MMHC is online-only, so the experience is built for teletherapy from the ground up.

Benefits of teletherapy for NYC residents

In NYC, “making time” can feel impossible. Teletherapy helps remove the friction that keeps support out of reach.

  • Skip the commute and save time (and energy).
  • More privacy—no waiting rooms, no running into coworkers.
  • Easier consistency when your week is unpredictable.
  • Meet from anywhere you can speak freely—home, office, or a quiet room.

When therapy fits your life, it’s easier to show up—and that’s where change starts.

How MMHC teletherapy works

The hard part shouldn’t be finding the right person. We guide you toward a good fit.

  1. Share your needs: what you want help with, preferences, schedule, insurance.
  2. Get matched with a licensed therapist from our team of 80+.
  3. Meet online—from NYC or anywhere in New York State.

If you need a different fit, we’ll help you adjust. Therapy should feel collaborative, not like another dead end.

Getting started (without overthinking it)

You don’t need the perfect explanation of what you’re feeling. You just need a starting point.

  • Tell us what’s been hardest lately
  • Share any preferences (style, identity, schedule, focus areas)
  • We’ll help with insurance questions and next steps

Momentum matters. Same-week appointments are often available, depending on schedules.

FAQ: Teletherapy NYC

Is MMHC teletherapy available in NYC?
Yes. MMHC provides online-only therapy to clients in NYC and across New York State, as long as you’re located in NYS for sessions.
Do you offer in-person sessions in Manhattan or other boroughs?
MMHC is an online-only practice. All sessions are held via secure telehealth.
How do I know if teletherapy is a good fit for me?
If commuting, time constraints, or privacy concerns make in-person therapy harder to sustain, teletherapy can be a strong fit. We’ll help you think it through when you reach out.
Do you take insurance for teletherapy?
We accept most major insurance and can help you confirm coverage before you begin.
How soon can I start?
Same-week appointments are often available, depending on therapist schedules and your preferences. We’ll help you find the earliest good-fit option.
