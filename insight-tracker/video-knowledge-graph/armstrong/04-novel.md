# Truly Striking & Novel Thoughts

The conversation between David Senra and Brian Armstrong includes several striking and novel thoughts that can change how someone thinks about entrepreneurship, innovation, and the future of finance. Here are a few examples:

* The idea that blockchain technology and cryptocurrency can create a more decentralized, global, and permissionless financial system, which can increase economic freedom.
* The concept of epigenetic reprogramming and its potential to revolutionize the field of medicine and improve human healthspan.
* The importance of perseverance, hard work, and learning from failures in achieving success, and how these qualities can be applied to various areas of life.
