How similarity is computed: Composite metric combining (1) Jaccard word overlap (35%), (2) sentence retention rate (35%), (3) position weighting — early sentences matter more (15%), and (4) critical term preservation — high-frequency words retained (15%). This penalizes heavy sentence loss that Jaccard alone would miss. Efficiency: semantic_score / retained_%. Higher means more meaning per character.
