Why online therapy works

Finding time for therapy can be the hardest part—especially when you’re already juggling work, family, and burnout.

  • No travel time or waiting rooms—just log in and start.
  • More consistency when your schedule shifts week to week.
  • Comfort matters: many people open up more from a familiar space.
  • Access statewide: you can work with a New York State therapist even if you’re not near a big city.

MMHC is online-only, so the process is designed around telehealth from the start—simple, clear, and supportive.

FAQ: Online therapist New York

Do I need to live in NYC to see an online therapist with MMHC?
No. MMHC provides online-only therapy to clients across New York State, as long as you’re located in NYS for sessions.
Is online therapy as “real” as in-person therapy?
Yes—online therapy is a full therapy session, just delivered via secure telehealth. Many people prefer it because it removes travel and makes consistency easier.
How quickly can I get an appointment?
Same-week appointments are often available, depending on schedules and your preferences. We’ll help you find the earliest good-fit option.
Do you take insurance for online therapy?
MMHC accepts most major insurance. When you reach out, we’ll help you confirm coverage and understand next steps.
What if I don’t know what kind of therapist I need?
That’s common. Share what you’re dealing with and what you want to be different, and we’ll match you with a licensed therapist aligned with your goals and preferences.
