Referral Eligibility

Clear Boundaries, Safe Referrals

Knowing who we serve well — and who we don't — protects your patients.

We Work With Adults Experiencing

  • ✓ Anxiety and panic disorders
  • ✓ Depression and mood disorders
  • ✓ Pregnancy, postpartum, and fertility-related stress
  • ✓ Chronic illness adjustment
  • ✓ GI-related stress and somatic symptoms
  • ✓ Trauma-related symptoms
  • ✓ Relationship and interpersonal stress
  • ✓ Grief and life transitions

Not Currently Equipped For

  • ✕ Active psychosis
  • ✕ Immediate hospitalization needs
  • ✕ Court-mandated treatment
  • ✕ Acute medical detox

Unsure about appropriateness? Reach out — we're happy to discuss before you refer.

Specialty Collaboration

Built for Your Practice Context

We work across specialties. Here's how we fit into your clinical workflow.

Women's Health & OB/GYN

Your 15-minute visit can't address perinatal mood disorders or fertility-related distress.

We pick up where you leave off — structured weekly sessions for pregnancy, postpartum, reproductive transitions, and perinatal mood, with confirmation back to your office.

Primary Care

You're screening positive for anxiety and depression but have nowhere reliable to send patients.

We accept the referral, verify insurance, and schedule — so your PHQ-9 follow-up actually leads somewhere instead of a handoff into the void.

GI & Pain Management

Your patients know stress worsens their symptoms, but "see a therapist" isn't a care plan.

We provide behavioral health support specifically for chronic symptoms, gut-brain axis concerns, and stress-related exacerbation — with coordination back to you.

Psychiatry

Medication management alone isn't enough, but finding a therapist who will actually coordinate is a nightmare.

We deliver structured weekly therapy alongside your prescribing — collaborative care, not parallel treatment. You'll know what's happening in sessions.
