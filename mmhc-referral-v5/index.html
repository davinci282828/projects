Our Referral Model

What Happens After You Refer

Most referral breakdown happens between "Here's a number to call" and "The patient actually starts care." Our model is designed specifically to prevent that gap.
01

You Submit, We Take It From Here

Send a referral via our secure form or email. That's the only step you need to take.

02

Patient Outreach Within 24 Hours

Our support team contacts the patient directly to initiate intake, answer questions, and begin the process.

This is our standard — every referral, every time.

03

Insurance Verification Before Scheduling

Insurance coverage is verified before scheduling — protecting your patient from surprise costs and protecting your practice from referral friction.

04

Clinical Matching

Clinical matching is based on presenting concern, medical context, treatment goals, and therapist expertise — not just availability.

05

Closed-Loop Confirmation

You'll receive confirmation when your patient is scheduled — and again after their first session is completed.

So you're never wondering whether care was initiated. Two confirmations: booked + seen.

Referral Eligibility

Clear Boundaries, Safe Referrals

Knowing who we serve well — and who we don't — protects your patients.

We Work With Adults Experiencing

  • Anxiety and panic disorders
  • Depression and mood disorders
  • Pregnancy, postpartum, and fertility-related stress
  • Chronic illness adjustment
  • GI-related stress and somatic symptoms
  • Trauma-related symptoms
  • Relationship and interpersonal stress
  • Grief and life transitions

Not Currently Equipped For

  • Active psychosis
  • Immediate hospitalization needs
  • Court-mandated treatment
  • Acute medical detox

Unsure about appropriateness? Reach out — we're happy to discuss before you refer.
